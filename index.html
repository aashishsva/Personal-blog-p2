Roadmap of Full Stack Web Development

A roadmap is a strategic plan that outlines the steps, milestones, and timeline needed to achieve a particular goal or complete a project. In the context of web development or technology, a roadmap might include:

  • Learning Path: A sequence of technologies, languages, or tools to learn, along with resources and timelines.
  • Project Plan: A detailed outline of the stages of a project, including design, development, testing, and deployment phases.
  • Career Goals: A plan for career development, including skills to acquire, certifications to obtain, and positions to target.
  • Product Development: A plan for developing a product, including feature development, testing, and release schedules.
